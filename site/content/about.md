# About

Most people meet Umamusume as a joke. Cute horse girls, gacha, memes; a parody you are not meant to take seriously.

Then, for a lot of people, something breaks. The fandom sometimes calls it the breaking-illusion point: the moment you realize this was never a joke. That every character is built on a real horse, a real life, a real record, a real death, and that the writers took all of it seriously, down to the grief. It is not a parody. It is not an idol story. It is real, and there is a soul and a story behind every story.

After that, you stop being a fan for the cuteness and the memes. You become a fan for the soul.

This site is built for that moment, and for everyone still standing on the other side of it. Every study here is one long argument that the depth is real, made from the original text and taken as seriously as the people who wrote it.

## What this is

UmaStudies is a one-person project: deep, primary-text character studies of the cast of *Umamusume: Pretty Derby*.

Every study is built from the original Japanese, character stories, support card stories, the career scenario, home-screen lines, alongside the real-horse record behind each character. The Japanese is treated as the authority text; the English is a working translation.

## Spoilers

These are complete studies. They discuss each character's full story, endings included. If you would rather experience a character's story in-game first, read their study afterward.

## A note on pace

I'm a student, and this runs alongside university work, a job, and other projects. I'm aiming for roughly one character a week, but please don't expect a fixed schedule. Each study takes real time to do properly, and I would rather post something with depth than something on time.

## Corrections welcome

I'm not a native Japanese speaker. If you spot a mistranslation, or have a reading you want to share, the feedback form at the bottom of each study comes straight to me.
